## Tips for Effective Outreach

1. **Personalize every email** — Reference a specific article they wrote. Generic pitches get deleted.
2. **Lead with value, not your product** — The guest post should be genuinely useful to their audience.
3. **Keep emails short** — Under 200 words in the body. Editors scan fast.
4. **Follow up once** — 5-7 days later. No response after that = move on.
5. **Offer the quiz widget as a standalone value prop** — It gives them free interactive content AND creates a natural backlink to you.
6. **Target 20-30 sites per week** — Expect a 10-15% response rate. Quality over quantity.
7. **Track in a spreadsheet** — Site, contact, date pitched, follow-up date, status, live URL.
8. **Prioritize DA 30+ sites** — Higher domain authority = more SEO value per backlink.
